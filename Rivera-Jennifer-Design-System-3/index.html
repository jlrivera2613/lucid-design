Client: Happening

Happening is an event-production company that puts together concerts, parties, shows, and more. They are focused on promoting both their events and their services as second-to-none for a wide variety of brands and clients. Their image is energetic, youthful, and fun.

Why "Lucid"?

Lucidity can be used to describe something that is clear and easy to understand which is the aim of this design system. The word lucid can also refer to something that is bright and luminous which is what I hope the look and feel of this design encompasses.
